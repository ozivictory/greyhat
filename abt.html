Goal of GHCS

GHCS is a registered company under the federal government of Nigeria with company number RC:2890850.

The goal of GHCS is to implement standard security measures on African cyberspace. Our mission relies mainly on making provisions for all devices connected to the cyber space using crowd sourced programs.

We value the African cyber space to extent of providing defensive mechanisms using crowd sourced programs and integrated machine learning algorithms in providing solutions to cyber related problems.

Knowing that the African cyberspace lacks adequate cybersecurity personels, GHCS has charged itself in the area of Training individuals about the concept of cybersecurity and also providing jobs for ethical hackers in the African cyberspace
